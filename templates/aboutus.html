About us

We are a cohort of 8 people. Team TRITONS, India, with different skills and ideologies, came together with the mission of cleaning the planet's water by trying to eradicate plastic waste and want to see aqua life flourishing.

We will also be doing various activities apart from cleaning, i.e., recycling plastic, drinking water (r.o treatment ), packaged flavored water, vermicompost for farmers.

We enjoy seeing our Vicinity with more blues and greens around us and are passionate about our mission. We are big fans of a healthy ecosystem that always says "yes" to Collaborations with environment cleaning agencies.
